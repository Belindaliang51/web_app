Correlations Between Weather and Bikeshare trip

Insights:

- Click on the axis to show different data(Y-axis first then X-axis)

- Observing the correlation between Temp and Ave Duration, it is easy to find out the trip duration grown as the temperature reached to 20-25 degrees. Similarly the growth of the bike using frequency grow up as temperature rises.

- However if we choose to observe the correlation between Temperature and average distance travelled, we can find the longest distance travelled (1322m) is under the temperature range of 0-5 degrees.
